ABOUT THE STATE OF ART

The State of Art was founded in 2021. The comprehensive product offering targets a complete spectrum for refined lovers of art. Our mission is to offer our customers unique brands enabling you to experience different art products and mediums not previously available in South Africa. Stocking premium consumables for the discerning artist and providing solutions to art challenges. We offer practical and functional online shopping making the experience of art supply procurement accessible and within reach. Through the use of established and reputable e-commerce, distribution is straight forward. Art Monarchs provides enduring value, engaging you and your art. Inspiring you to create and be creative using your artistic license with distinction. Should we not have what you are looking for, let us know and we can source these products for you.
